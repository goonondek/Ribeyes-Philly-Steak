Our Mobile Kitchen

We bring the Philly experience to you through our custom-built food truck.

Our 28-foot custom-built food truck features a hickory wood smoker, professional flat-top grill, and all the equipment needed to deliver authentic Philly cheesesteaks on the go. We're fully licensed and insured to serve throughout the Austin area.

A Message from the Owner

James Wilson

Founder & Head Chef

"After moving from Philadelphia to Austin, I missed the authentic cheesesteaks from my hometown. In 2018, I decided to bring that taste to Texas using premium ribeye steak and traditional techniques. What started as a small food truck has grown into Austin's favorite Philly cheesesteak spot. Every sandwich is made with the same care I'd give to my own family."

Our Philosophy

Quality Ingredients

We use only USDA Prime ribeye steak, fresh Amoroso rolls imported from Philadelphia, and premium white American cheese. Our vegetables are locally sourced when possible.

Authentic Techniques

We chop our steak on the grill with onions, just like they do in Philadelphia. Our secret? A perfectly seasoned flat-top grill and the right spatula technique.

Community Commitment

We partner with local schools and charities, and we're proud to participate in Austin's vibrant food truck community. 5% of all profits go to local food banks.
